Muhammad Ali Jinnah

1876 - 1948

Founder of Pakistan

Muhammad Ali Jinnah was a barrister, politician, and the founder of Pakistan. Muhammad Ali Jinnah served as the leader of the All-India Muslim League from 1913 until the inception of Pakistan on 14 August 1947, and then as the Dominion of Pakistan's first governor-general until his death.
